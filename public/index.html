Lesson tracks

MasterWrite lessons come in six language pathways.

Use the globe icon in the header to switch tracks any time. Each lesson pathway helps learners practise grammar, punctuation, sentence flow, and vocabulary in a language they use every day.

Core features

Designed to teach, not just correct.

The app focuses on helping users understand what changed, why it changed, and how to write stronger sentences next time.

Grammar and punctuation repair

Detects common writing issues such as lowercase sentence starts, spacing mistakes, repeated words, weak endings, and missing punctuation.

Advanced vocabulary suggestions

Offers stronger word choices based on the selected writing mode so users can sound clearer, more academic, or more polished.

Essay analysis

Measures sentence count, average sentence length, reading time, lexical variety, and paragraph structure to guide improvement.

Academic scoring

Generates an easy-to-understand performance score across clarity, punctuation, structure, and vocabulary strength.
